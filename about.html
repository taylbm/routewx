Story

Route Weather was created by Brandon Taylor, when his Aunt Jill asked why an app didn't exist to get travel forecasts in navigation apps.

  • The initial JavaScript version was released in early 2019.
  • In 2020, Brandon began development on the iOS version.
  • After 6 months of testing the initial beta version, the production version was released on August 15, 2021.
  • Swift Weather Solutions LLC was incorporated on September 26, 2021, which is the startup associated with the iOS app.

Future plans for Route Weather include incorporating the forecast data directly into a turn-by-turn navigation interface, and adding animated data layers, including MRMS radar data.
